bio

Hi, I'm Nick!

I go to Skyline High School (Class of 2023), and I love playing the clarinet, listening to classical music, and writing code.

I am currently in the IB (International Baccalaureate) program at Skyline. I also founded the Skyline Coding Club in Fall 2019 with my friends Grant and Evan.

Sometimes, I make woodwind covers of popular film/video game music for YouTube. I began playing the clarinet when I was 5 years old, and I can also play the alto saxophone, flute, piano, and tin whistle. My favorite composer at the moment is Dmitri Shostakovich.

I've written code since I was 10 years old.
